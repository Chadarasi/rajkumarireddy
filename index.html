About Us

RK Tours & Travels has been serving customers for over 30 years, offering affordable, comfortable, and memorable travel experiences across South India.

Meet the Owner: Rajkumari Reddy — With three decades of experience in the travel industry, I am passionate about creating journeys that leave lasting memories. My mission is to ensure every client feels safe, valued, and excited about their trip.

Our services include customized tours, holiday packages, and reliable transport options. We proudly serve Rajahmundry, Jangareddygudem, Nidadavolu, Polavaram, and Kovvuru.
